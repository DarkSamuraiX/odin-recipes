Lasagna Recipe

Description

Home made Lasagna in 10 min work without any effort

Lasagna Indredients

  • Meat
  • Onion & Gralic
  • Tomato
  • Suger
  • Spices and season
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Making Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
